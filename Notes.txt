Users can:
    -create new topics in the '/topics/new' page
    -view all topics in the '/topics' pgae, from which they can click on individual topics and go to them after being
    redirected to the page for that specific topics 
        -viewed from '/topics' page 
    -view an individual topic and all quizzes for that topic. Users can click on a quiz associated with a topic and can 
    be redirected to that quiz's page 
        -viewed from the '/topics/:topicID' page
    -create quizzes that are associated with topics and contain lists of flashcards. Users can add and remove card fields
    in the quiz form 
        -this can be done on the 'quizzes/new' page
    -view all quizzes and click on individual quizzes to be redirected to their individual pages 
        -viewed from '/quizzes' page 
    -can view individual quizzes and and flip cards over 
        -viewed from the '/quizzes/:quizId' page
